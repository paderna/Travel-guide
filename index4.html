Aristocrats

146 Jupiter St Bel Air Village, Makati, Luzon Philippines

 


Aristocrats

The Aristocrat Restaurant is a popular Filipino restaurant chain with several locations throughout Metro Manila, including in Makati. The restaurant has been in operation since 1936 and is known for its classic Filipino dishes, including its famous chicken barbecue, which has become one of the restaurant's signature dishes. Apart from its chicken barbecue, other popular dishes at the Aristocrat Restaurant include "sinigang na baboy" (sour pork soup), "kare-kare" (oxtail stew in peanut sauce), and "pancit palabok" (rice noodles in shrimp sauce). The restaurant also offers a range of other Filipino favorites, such as "adobo," "sisig," and "lechon kawali." The Aristocrat Restaurant in Makati is located in the heart of the central business district, making it a convenient dining option for locals and visitors alike. The restaurant features a casual and comfortable atmosphere, with indoor and outdoor seating options available. Overall, the Aristocrat Restaurant is a beloved institution in the Philippines, known for its delicious food and warm hospitality. It's definitely worth checking out if you're in Makati or any other area where the restaurant is located. .

Bistro Remedios

1911 M. Adriatico Street Malate, Manila, Luzon Philippines

 


Bistro Remedios

Bistro Remedios is a popular Filipino restaurant located in Makati City, Philippines. It is known for serving traditional Filipino cuisine in a rustic and charming setting reminiscent of old-world Philippines. The restaurant is located on the ground floor of the Remedios Circle in Malate, Manila and is a sister restaurant to the famous Cafe Adriatico. Some of the must-try dishes at Bistro Remedios include crispy pata, kare-kare, sinigang na hipon, and adobo. Bistro Remedios also features live entertainment with traditional Filipino music and dance performances on certain nights, adding to its unique and lively dining experience. It is a great place to experience authentic Filipino food and culture in Makati City .

Cafe adreatico

1790 M. Adriatico Street Malate, Manila.


 


Cafe adreatico

Cafe Adriatico is a popular restaurant located in Manila, Philippines. It is known for its traditional Filipino cuisine and old-world charm, with a cozy ambiance that transports diners back in time. The restaurant has been a fixture in Manila's food scene for over 30 years and is known for its extensive menu that features Filipino dishes such as adobo, sinigang, kare-kare, and crispy pata, as well as international favorites like pasta and sandwiches. Aside from its delicious food, Cafe Adriatico is also known for its unique decor that features vintage memorabilia, photographs, and artworks, giving it a distinct character and charm. The restaurant also has a bar that serves a variety of cocktails and drinks, making it a great place to hang out with friends or family. Overall, Cafe Adriatico is a must-visit for anyone looking for a taste of traditional Filipino cuisine in a cozy and charming setting in Manila.

SHAWARMA SNACK

Shawarma Snack Center is a popular fast-food chain in the Philippines that specializes in serving shawarma, a Middle Eastern dish made with grilled meat, vegetables, and spices wrapped in pita bread. The chain has various locations throughout the country and is known for its affordable prices and delicious shawarma options. Some of the most popular dishes at Shawarma Snack Center include beef and chicken shawarma, shawarma rice, and shawarma fries. Aside from shawarma, the chain also offers other Middle Eastern-inspired dishes such as falafel, kebab, and hummus. Customers can also customize their orders with a variety of sauces and toppings to suit their taste. Overall, Shawarma Snack Center is a great option for anyone looking for a quick and delicious shawarma fix in the Philippines. The chain's affordable prices and wide range of menu options make it a popular choice among locals and tourists alike .

Bario fiesta

Eng bee tin

518 Quintin Paredes Street Binondo, Manila, Luzon Philippines..


 

Eng bee tin

Eng Bee Tin is a popular Chinese deli and pastry shop located in Binondo, Manila, Philippines. The shop is known for its wide variety of traditional Chinese snacks and delicacies, including hopia (a type of pastry filled with sweet bean paste or other fillings), tikoy (a sticky rice cake), mooncakes, and other treats. One of the most popular items at Eng Bee Tin is its hopia, which comes in a variety of flavors such as ube (purple yam), mongo (mung bean), baboy (pork), and even pizza. The shop also offers other Chinese delicacies such as tikoy in different flavors and mooncakes during the Mid-Autumn Festival. Eng Bee Tin has a cozy and nostalgic atmosphere, with traditional Chinese decor that creates a warm and welcoming environment. It is a great place to go for a quick snack or to buy souvenirs for friends and family. Overall, Eng Bee Tin is a must-visit for anyone looking for traditional Chinese snacks and delicacies in Manila. Its wide variety of treats, delicious flavors, and nostalgic atmosphere make it a popular choice among locals and tourists alike..
